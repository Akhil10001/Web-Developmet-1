The Time I Have Realised That Life Is Unpredictable

  • About
  • Education

Recently a favorite actor of mine has passed away suddenly. He was extremely fit and a very positive person. He died of a heart attack at his house during a workout session. If you saw him in life, you would not think he would die of a heart attack, but it all happened in a minute.

I have realized that life is too short to live without happiness. We really cannot predict when we will die. I have also learned that I should thank everyone who helped me to be whatever I am today and tell the people I love the most that I will always love them no matter what. I have been thinking about how I want to live my life and how I want to leave the earth.
