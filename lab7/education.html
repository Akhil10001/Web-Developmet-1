Stevens Institute of Technology

The class I like the most is CS-546. I have always wanted to pursue Web development as a career option but I did not know where to start and what technologies should I be familiar. But, because of this course I have learned a lot more than I thought I would. Now, I have understood how to move forward in web development and make it as my career.

The most memorable day was attending my first CS-546 lecture, I remember professor Hill was explaining about the rules that we have to follow for the class and cracking jokes in between and trying to make us concentrate on the rules and what we are going to learn in the semester.
